Healthcare Without Boundaries

The Health Innovation at Kaiser Permanente website was built on WordPress while I was at Urban Influence. The CMS uses drag and drop modules allowing them to modify the layout and content of their website whenever their content needs to be updated. This system also allows them to build pages on their own without any development knowledge. The team also wanted something reflective of their innovative thinking, so I worked with their in-house illustrator to come up with a lot of the hand drawn goodness you see throughout the website.
